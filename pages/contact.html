Common Questions

Quick Answers

How much does CCTV installation cost in Los Angeles? +

Pricing depends on the number of cameras, cable runs, recording system, and property type. A basic 4-camera residential system starts around $800–$1,200 installed. Commercial systems with 8–16 cameras typically run $2,500–$6,000+. We provide a detailed written quote after a free site assessment.

Do you offer maintenance contracts? +

Yes. We offer monthly and annual service agreements for businesses that need guaranteed response times, regular health checks, and firmware updates. Contact us to discuss options for your property.

Can you work on our existing system? +

Often yes. We service and repair many brands and can add cameras or users to most existing CCTV and access control systems. Send us the make and model and we'll let you know upfront.

How long does installation take? +

Small systems (4–8 cameras or 1–4 doors) are typically done in one day. Larger commercial projects are scheduled across multiple days. We give you a realistic timeline in the proposal — we don't rush and compromise quality.

Are you licensed and insured? +

Yes. SDA Integrations holds a C-7 Low-Voltage Contractor license from the California State License Board (CSLB) and carries full general liability insurance. We can provide proof of insurance for any project that requires it.
