Credential Types

Every Access Method, One System

Modern access control doesn't mean picking one technology. We design systems that support multiple credential types — so different staff, visitors, or residents can use what works for them.

  • Key fobs and proximity cards (HID, 125kHz, 13.56MHz)
  • PIN keypad entry — standalone or combined with cards
  • Biometric readers — fingerprint and facial recognition
  • Mobile credentials — smartphone Bluetooth / NFC
  • Intercom + camera integration for visitor management
  • License plate recognition for vehicle gate control
  • Multi-factor authentication for high-security areas

System Management

Full Control from Any Device

Cloud-based and on-premise management platforms let you add or remove users, review access logs, and lock or unlock doors remotely — from your phone or desktop.

  • Web and mobile app management
  • Real-time access event logging with timestamps
  • Scheduled access — doors lock/unlock automatically
  • Instant credential revocation (lost fob, terminated employee)
  • Multi-site management from one dashboard
  • Integration with CCTV for event-triggered recording
  • Reports: who entered, when, and for how long
