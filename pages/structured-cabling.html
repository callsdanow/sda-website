Cabling Types

Every Cable Type, Every Application

We work with the full range of low-voltage cabling standards and help you choose the right infrastructure for your bandwidth needs and budget.

  • Cat5e — reliable for 1Gbps up to 100 meters
  • Cat6 — ideal for most commercial applications
  • Cat6A — 10Gbps, future-proof for high-density offices
  • Single-mode fiber optic — inter-building and long runs
  • Multi-mode fiber — high-speed intra-building backbone
  • Coax cabling — CATV and existing camera systems
  • Speaker and AV low-voltage cabling
