FAQ

Common Questions

How much does commercial security camera installation cost in Los Angeles? +

Commercial security camera installation in Los Angeles typically starts around $1,500–$2,500 for a basic 4–8 camera system with NVR. Larger commercial properties with 16–32+ cameras run $4,000–$12,000+, depending on camera type, cable runs, and recording infrastructure. We provide a free on-site assessment and written quote before any work begins.

What is the cost of access control installation for a commercial building? +

Access control installation for a single door in Los Angeles typically starts at $800–$1,500, including reader, controller, and electric strike or magnetic lock. Multi-door commercial systems range from $2,500 for a small office to $15,000+ for a large building with 10+ doors, parking gates, and a management platform. We offer both standalone and cloud-managed systems.

Do you install Ubiquiti UniFi, Verkada, or Alcatraz AI systems? +

Yes. We install and configure Ubiquiti UniFi Access and UniFi Protect, Verkada cameras and access control, Alcatraz AI facial recognition access control, and many other platforms including HID, LenelS2, Honeywell, Genetec, Hikvision, Dahua, Axis, Schlage, Avigilon, and Cisco Meraki. We work with the platform that fits your needs and budget.

Do you provide security camera repair and access control maintenance? +

Yes. We offer CCTV maintenance, security camera repair, access control maintenance, and access control repair for both systems we installed and existing third-party systems. We also offer service contracts for commercial properties that need scheduled maintenance and guaranteed response times.
